CATALYST CORE: RULES REFERENCE

CHARACTER CREATION

Step 1: Classification (choose one)
- Mutant: Reroll one failed saving throw per long rest.
- Enhanced Human: Advantage on Technology checks.
- Magic User: Cast one minor magical effect once per long rest.
- Alien/Extraterrestrial: Immune to environmental hazards; ignore rough terrain penalties.
- Mystical Being: +2 to Persuasion or Intimidation checks.

Step 2: Power Style (choose up to 2, only 1 perk applies)
- Physical Powerhouse: Halve one attack’s damage once per combat encounter.
- Energy Manipulator: Reroll 1s once per turn.
- Speedster: +10 ft movement and +1 TC when moving 20+ ft.
- Telekinetic/Psychic: Once per rest, force enemies to reroll rolls of 18–20.
- Illusionist: Create a 1-minute decoy once per combat encounter.
- Shape-shifter: Advantage on Deception; disguise freely.
- Elemental Controller: +2 to hit and +5 damage once per turn with elemental powers.

Step 3: Origin Story (choose one)
- Accident: Resistance to one damage type.
- Experiment: Reroll failed CON/INT save once per long rest.
- Legacy: Use another character’s power once per long rest.
- Awakening: +5 to hit, +10 damage when below 1/2 HP.
- Pact: Auto-success on one save or +10 to any roll per long rest.
- Lost Time: Once per combat, declare a Skill Move (free, +1d6 effect).
- Exposure: +5 elemental damage once per round.
- Rebirth: If knocked out, revive with 1 HP + damage resistance for 1 round.
- Vigil: Once per combat, shield all allies from damage for 1 round.
- Redemption: Once/day take damage for ally, they heal 1d6 + gain advantage.

Step 4: Ability Scores
- Roll 7d20, drop lowest. Assign to STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA.

Step 5: Toughness Class (TC)
- Formula: 10 + DEX modifier + Armor/Shield/Origin bonuses.

Step 6: Hit Points (HP)
- Formula: 30 + CON modifier + (Tier × 1d10).
- Rookie (Tier 5): +1d10
- Emerging Vigilante (Tier 4): +2d10
- Field-Tested (Tier 3): +3d10
- Respected Force (Tier 2): +4d10
- Heroic Figure (Tier 1): +5d10
- Transcendent (Tier 0): Fixed 100 + CON

At 0 HP:
- Roll 3 CON saves DC 13. 2+ successes = stabilize at 1 HP. Fail = unconscious.

Step 7: Powers
- Create 3–5 custom powers.
- Define: Name, Range, Effect, SP Cost, Save (if any), Description.

Step 8: Stamina Points (SP)
- Max SP = 5 + CON modifier.
- SP fully regenerates at start of each combat round.
- Power costs: 1–5 SP.

Step 9: Alignment
Two Axes:
- Moral: Light, Neutral, Shadow.
- Discipline: Lawful, Neutral, Chaotic.
Each combo grants unique perk.

CORE MECHANICS

Turn Structure
- Each turn: 1 Action, 1 Movement, 1 Reaction. Some allow Bonus Actions.

Initiative
- Roll 1d20 + DEX modifier.

Attack Rolls
- 1d20 + modifiers vs TC.

Critical Hits
- Nat 20 = double damage dice.

Saving Throws
- STR: Grapples, knockback, force.
- DEX: Dodging AoE, explosions, speed.
- CON: Poison, radiation, endurance.
- INT: Illusions, hacks, disruption.
- WIS: Psychic, sensory overload.
- CHA: Emotional manipulation.

SP Cost Guide
- 1 SP: Basic attack, minor effect.
- 2 SP: Core ability, status effect.
- 3 SP: AoE, heal, enhanced status.
- 4 SP: Strong AoE, control.
- 5 SP: Ultimate (10-turn cooldown).

Cinematic Action Point
- 1 per session. May:
  • Auto-succeed a roll.
  • Interrupt initiative.
  • Flashback: +5 to a roll.
  • Redirect damage/rescue ally.

Downtime System
- Media Control (CHA): Influence public trust.
- Research (INT/WIS): Learn threat weaknesses.
- Train/Tinker (STR/INT): +1 SP or upgrade.
- Gather Intel (CHA/WIS): Secrets, traps.
- Personal Time (WIS/CHA): Refresh; reroll save next session.

Resistances & Vulnerabilities
- Mutant: Resist radiation/psychic. Weak necrotic/force.
- Enhanced Human: Resist piercing/fire. Weak psychic/radiation.
- Magic User: Resist force/necrotic. Weak confusion/radiation.
- Alien: Resist cold/acid/lightning. Weak radiant/emotion.
- Mystical: Resist radiant/psychic. Weak corruption/radiation.

EQUIPMENT

Gear Types: Armor, Shields, Utility.
Rarity: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Elite, Legendary.

Universal Gear
- Common: +1 TC, minor bonuses (stealth, fall dmg resist).
- Uncommon: +2 TC, resistances (slashing, fire/cold).
- Rare: +3 TC, advantage on saves, special effects.
- Elite: +3 TC, regen or terrain immunity.
- Legendary: +4 TC, powerful effects (auto-block, teleport).

Class-Specific Gear
- Physical Powerhouse: Heavy armor, melee shields, tremor boots, etc.
- Energy Manipulator: Energy armor, SP-charging gear, reflectors.
- Speedster: Velocity suits, dash-boost gear, initiative bonuses.
- Telekinetic/Psychic: Mind armor, reflection shields, psionic nodes.
- Illusionist: Stealth cloaks, decoy gear, disorienters.
- Shape-shifter: Morph armor, clone shields, regen gear.
- Elemental Controller: Elemental ward gear, fire/cold/earth resistances.

Equip Limit: 1 Armor, 1 Shield, 1 Utility (unless specified).

FACTION REPUTATION

Factions: O.M.N.I., PFV, Cosmic Conclave, Greyline.
- Track influence as “Tiers.”
- Actions raise or lower reputation.
- Reputation impacts NPC reactions, mission options, and resource access.

FAQ

Q1: How do Stamina Points regenerate?
A1: SP fully regenerates at the start of each combat round.

Q2: What happens at 0 HP?
A2: Roll 3 CON saves (DC 13). Success = stabilize at 1 HP. Failure = unconscious.

Q3: How many powers can I create?
A3: 3–5, using SP cost chart.

Q4: What is a Signature Move?
A4: Your most iconic ability (2–3 SP), tied to your Origin/identity.

Q5: Can I mix Power Styles?
A5: Yes. Only the primary grants the perk.

Q6: What are Cinematic Action Points?
A6: 1 per session. Auto-succeed, flashback, interrupt initiative, or rescue.

Q7: What is TC?
A7: Toughness Class. Defense rating. 10 + DEX + bonuses.

Q8: How do alignments affect play?
A8: They grant perks and guide roleplay.

Q9: Do gear bonuses stack with powers?
A9: Yes, unless explicitly stated otherwise.

Q10: What are ultimate powers?
A10: Cost 5 SP, 10-turn cooldown, very strong.

Q11: What dice system is used?
A11: Mostly d20. Damage is usually d6 unless stated.

Q12: How do resistances/vulnerabilities apply?
A12: Resistance halves damage. Vulnerability doubles it.

Q13: How many items can I equip?
A13: One armor, one shield, one utility.

Q14: What happens if I fail all 3 death saves?
A14: You die permanently. Three successes instead leave you stable at 0 HP. A natural 20 restores 1 HP and consciousness, while a natural 1 counts as two failures.

Q15: Do downtime activities matter?
A15: Yes. They provide bonuses, intel, or rerolls.

Q16: How does reputation impact play?
A16: High/low faction reputation alters story paths, allies, and resources.

Q17: Can I heal during combat?
A17: Yes, via powers, gear, or ally abilities.

Q18: Can SP be saved between combats?
A18: No. SP resets to max each new combat round.

Q19: What happens on a critical hit?
A19: Double damage dice are rolled.

Q20: Can multiple resistances stack?
A20: No. Only one applies unless GM allows.
