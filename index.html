Resonance Points (RP)

Resonance Points are awarded by the GM for truly heroic acts. At 5 RP, trigger a Heroic Surge for temporary boosts.

0

Heroic Surge Effects

  • Roleplay checks: +2 to CHA/WIS/INT (leadership, persuasion, interpretation)
  • Combat: +1d4 to all attack rolls and saving throws
  • SP Flow: +1 SP regenerated per round
  • Bonus XP: grant at session end (GM)

Duration: 1 encounter or 10 minutes of narrative time

Aftermath: first save at disadvantage; first round next combat regenerate 1 fewer SP

          Welcome to the Tracker

          Use the tabs to navigate your sheet. Changes save automatically and work offline.

          If you're new here, register to save your character and access cloud features.

          How to Use the Tracker

          • Theme toggleSwitch between light, high-contrast, and dark modes using the sun, contrast, or moon icons.
          • Main menuTap the menu icon to open options—Log In, Character wizard, Encounter tracker, Save, Roll/Flip Log, Campaign Log, Rules, Help, and Players.
          • Tab navigationUse Combat, Abilities, Powers, Gear, and Story icons to move between sections.
          • Combat toolsAdjust HP/SP, track death saves, initiative, defenses, and conditions; roll dice or flip coins directly.
          • Abilities managementEdit ability scores and proficiency bonuses on the Abilities tab.
          • Powers managementRecord powers or spells and mark off uses in the Powers tab.
          • Gear management & catalogTrack equipment and currency on the Gear tab; open the gear catalog to search items.
          • Story notesUse the Story tab to store background details, quests, and other notes.
          • Character creation wizardBuild a new character step by step from the main menu.
          • Encounter trackerManage combatant lists and turn order with Add, Prev, Next, and Reset controls.
          • Dice rolling & coin flipsUse the Roll/Flip Log to perform rolls or flips and review results.
          • Campaign & roll logsKeep adventure notes and roll history in their respective logs.
          • Rules referenceRead the Character Creation Guide from the Rules menu.
          • Player & DM loginLog in for cloud features or use the DM link to manage shared tools.
          • Save charactersSave locally or to the cloud after logging in.
          • Offline auto-savingChanges are stored in your browser and sync when you're back online.

          Frequently Asked Questions

          • How do I start a new character? Open the main menu and select Character wizard.
          • Can I use the tracker offline? Yes. The app auto-saves in your browser and syncs once you log in again.
          • How do I change the theme? Tap the sun, contrast, or moon icon at the top of the page.
          • Where do I adjust my HP or SP? Visit the Combat tab and tap the current values to edit them.
          • How do I roll a die or flip a coin? Open the Roll/Flip Log from the main menu and choose the die or coin you need.
          • How can I manage my equipment? Use the Gear tab and tap Open Gear Catalog to search and add items.
          • Is there a way to track initiative? Yes. Use the Encounter tracker to add combatants and move through turns.
          • What is the Campaign Log for? It stores session notes. Open it from the main menu to add or review entries.
          • How do I share my character with the DM? Log in and use Save to upload your character so the DM can access it.
          • Where can I find the rules? Tap Rules in the main menu to open the Character Creation Guide.
          • Can I see past rolls? The Roll/Flip Log keeps a history of your recent rolls, and the full log shows everything.
          • How do I log out? Open the main menu; once logged in the Log In button becomes Logout.
